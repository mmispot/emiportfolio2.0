Pandatama Release - 25/10/2024

A tamagotchi game I made for school. It has multiple actions, such as

    - A shop to buy items (and halloween outfits!)
    - A cooking mechanism
    - Picking berries from the garden
    - Switching between 4 backgrounds
    - Put your panda to sleep, wash it or play with it
    - Your panda shows different sprites based on its needs/stats
    - Your panda has different stats that all decrease with time
    - You can name your panda
    - The status, name and your inventory are all saved in localStorage
    - Different kinds of foods/drinks, all giving a different amount of satisfaction/health/hunger
    - And more!
